User Guide

Using HomeKonet — for Guests & Hosts

A complete walkthrough of every part of the HomeKonet app: finding and booking a place to stay, managing your trips, and — if you list a property — running your host account.

👋 Welcome to HomeKonet

HomeKonet is a real-estate booking platform connecting property owners ("hosts") with guests looking for short-term nightly stays or long-term monthly rentals across Liberia. You can browse listings without an account, but you'll need one to book, message, or list a property.

Two roles, one account Every account starts as a guest. Applying to become a host and getting approved adds hosting tools to the same account — you don't need a separate login to switch between booking trips and managing your own listings.

👤 Your Account

Creating an account

Sign up from the header "Sign up" button or the full-page /login screen. You'll need a first name, last name, email, and a password (at least 8 characters). You can also sign up with Google in one click.

After registering, check your email for a verification link — you won't be able to log in until your email is verified.

Logging in

Log in with your email/password or with Google. If your account has two-factor authentication turned on, you'll be asked for a 6-digit code from your authenticator app after your password is accepted.

Forgot your password? Use the "Forgot password?" link on the sign-in popup (click "Log in" from the header). Enter your email and you'll receive a reset link — follow it to set a new password.

Your profile

From Account, you can update your first/last name, email, a short bio, and your profile photo.

Two-factor authentication (2FA)

Under Account → Login & security, turn on two-factor authentication for extra protection:

  1. Click "Enable MFA" and scan the QR code with an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.), or enter the shown key manually.
  2. Enter the 6-digit code the app generates to confirm.
  3. Save the 8 backup codes you're shown — each works once if you ever lose access to your authenticator app. They're shown only this one time.

Once enabled, every login asks for a fresh code after your password. You can turn it off anytime from the same section (you'll need a current code to confirm).

Changing your Mobile Money number

Also from the Account page, under "Change Mobile Money Number":

  1. Enter your current password (skip this if you signed up with Google only), your new number, and your network (MTN or Orange).
  2. We send a 6-digit code to your email and the new number.
  3. Enter the code to confirm the change.

You can cancel a pending change before you confirm it if you entered the wrong number.

Deleting your account

The Account page has a "Danger zone" with a delete option. If you have upcoming bookings — as a guest, or on a listing you host — resolve or wait for them first; otherwise deletion is immediate: your account is deactivated, your personal details are anonymized, and any listings you own are unpublished. This can't be undone from within the app, so contact support first if you're not sure.

🔍 Browsing & Search

The home page and search page are how you find a place to stay.

Home page

Browse by category (Apartment, Hotels, Lodge, Beaches, Office Space, Land, and more), see listings near you (using your browser location), and read guest testimonials.

Searching

Open the search box from the header to set a location, check-in/check-out dates, property type, budget range, and guest count (adults, children, infants, pets). This takes you to the results page.

Filtering results

On the results page, use Filters to narrow further by price range, bedrooms/beds/bathrooms, amenities, and toggles for Instant Book and Superhost listings. Switch to map view to see listings plotted with their nightly/monthly price.

The listing page

Each listing shows a photo gallery, amenities, the host's profile (with a Superhost badge if they qualify), a live price breakdown, and guest reviews with ratings for cleanliness, accuracy, check-in, communication, location, and value. Hotel-type listings let you pick a specific room type with its own price and availability.

Saving favorites

Tap the heart icon on any listing to save it — view everything you've saved under Wishlists.

Saving a search & getting alerts

Found a search worth repeating? Click Save search in the results toolbar, give it a name, and choose how often you want to hear about new matches (instantly, daily, or weekly). Your saved searches — and any new listings that matched one — live under your Dashboard → Saved Searches tab, where you can re-run a search or remove it anytime.

Comparing properties

Click Compare in the results toolbar, then check up to 4 listings you're deciding between. When you've picked at least 2, tap the floating "Compare" button, name your comparison, and view it anytime under Dashboard → Comparisons — each property is scored with call-outs on what stands out (price, size, etc.) relative to the others in that comparison.

📅 Booking a Stay

How a booking works

  1. Request. Submit a booking request from the listing page — this step is free, nothing is charged yet.
  2. Confirmation. Some listings are Instant Book and confirm right away; others need the host to approve — hosts have up to 7 days to confirm a request.
  3. Payment. Once confirmed, you have 10 days to complete payment, or the reservation expires and the dates are released.
  4. Contact shared. After an admin confirms your payment, your host's contact details are shared so you can coordinate check-in.
Tip If a host doesn't confirm within 7 days, or you don't pay within 10 days of confirmation, the request simply expires automatically — you won't be charged for a booking that never completes.

Paying for your stay

From your booking's payment page, choose:

  • Card (Stripe) — enter your card details directly.
  • MTN Mobile Money — enter your MoMo phone number; you'll approve the charge on your phone, and the page checks for confirmation automatically. MTN charges its own 2% transaction fee on top of the total.

Viewing appointments (long-term rentals)

Monthly/long-term listings require a short in-person viewing before you can reserve. Pick an available Saturday and a 2-hour time block, then pay the (non-refundable) viewing fee to lock in the slot. Track your scheduled viewings under Viewings. Once a viewing is marked complete, you can reserve the property directly from that page and choose your lease start date.

🧳 Managing Your Trips

Your bookings — upcoming and past — live under Trips, with a summary also shown on your Dashboard.

Booking status

Each booking shows its current stage — awaiting host confirmation, awaiting your payment (with days remaining), payment received and awaiting admin confirmation, or fully confirmed.

Cancelling a booking

Use the Cancel button on a trip and confirm. Every listing has a cancellation policy set by its host (Flexible, Moderate, Strict, or Super Strict, shown on the listing page) — refund eligibility depends on that policy. If you're owed a refund, our team reviews and processes it; for the fastest resolution, message your host or open a support ticket referencing your booking.

Messaging your host

Use Messages to chat with your host (or a guest, if you're hosting). You can send file attachments once you have a confirmed booking together. For your safety, sharing contact details directly in chat is discouraged — going through official channels keeps you covered by our support and dispute process.

Filing an AirCover claim

If something goes wrong during or after a confirmed stay — property damage, missing items, a cleanliness issue, or a safety concern — click File a claim on that booking in Trips. Pick a category, describe what happened, and enter the amount you're requesting. Our team reviews every claim and follows up with next steps; an approved claim is paid out separately, not automatically at the moment it's approved.

Notifications

Check the notification bell for updates on every stage of your booking, new messages, price/availability changes on listings you're watching, and — if you're applying to host or verifying a property — updates on your application status. You can adjust which emails you receive from your Account page.

⭐ Reviews

Once your stay is complete, you can leave a review from the listing page or your Trips list. Give an overall rating plus optional scores for cleanliness, accuracy, check-in, communication, location, and value, and a written comment. You can leave one review per stay. Browse every review site-wide on the Reviews page, and see host replies published alongside guest reviews.

🏠 Becoming a Host

Apply from Become a Host (you'll need to be logged in).

What you'll submit

  • Your full legal name, address, and phone number
  • A headshot photo
  • A photo of a government-issued ID or passport
  • Agreement to the Property Owner Agreement

The review process

Every application goes through three sequential review stages, and you can track your progress on the application page:

  1. Product Support review
  2. Compliance review
  3. Supervisor review — final approval

You'll get a notification at each step. If your application is declined at any stage, you'll see the reason (if one was provided) and can apply again.

Agreement updates If we publish a new version of the Property Owner Agreement after you're approved, you'll need to accept it again before creating a new listing.

🗂️ Hosting on HomeKonet Host feature

Once approved, your Host Dashboard is your hosting control center.

Overview

See stat cards for your earnings, listings, bookings, and rating, plus earnings and bookings charts.

Properties

See your draft listings (continue editing or discard) and published listings. For each published listing you can Edit (title, description, photos, amenities, pricing, discounts, booking mode, cancellation policy, and safety details), manage hotel rooms if applicable, view the public listing page, or delete it (blocked while there are active bookings).

Bookings

Separate tabs for Upcoming, Accepted, Declined, and Past bookings. On a pending request you can Confirm or Decline (with an optional note to the guest).

Earnings

See your payouts split into "Awaiting payout" and "Paid," each showing the gross booking amount, HomeKonet's commission, and your net payout. Payouts are disbursed by the HomeKonet team once a guest's payment is confirmed — they're not instant.

Messages & Reviews

Reply to guest messages directly from the dashboard, and respond publicly to reviews on your listings from the Recent Reviews section.

Where to actually change your price The dashboard's Pricing section is a quick estimator to preview numbers — to make a real pricing change, open the listing itself under Properties → Edit and update it there.

➕ Creating & Managing Listings Host feature

Start a new listing from Host Dashboard → Properties → "Add listing." The wizard adapts to what you're listing:

Nightly / short-term stays

Property type, location, bedrooms/beds/bathrooms/guests, amenities, photos, title & description, highlights, nightly weekday/weekend pricing, discounts (last-minute, weekly, monthly), booking mode (Instant vs. Approve First), and cancellation policy.

Monthly / long-term rentals

Same basics, plus monthly rent, payment schedule (monthly/quarterly/biannual/annual), and lease term (6, 12, 24, or 36 months — long enough to cover your chosen payment schedule).

Your progress is saved automatically as a draft, so you can pick up a listing later without starting over.

Hotel listings

For hotel-type properties, define one or more room types during creation (name, type, price per night, occupancy, beds, bathrooms, per-room amenities, photos, and inventory count). Manage room types anytime from the listing's "Manage Rooms" screen — add, edit, deactivate, or remove a room type.

✅ Property Verification Host feature

Before a new listing goes live, you'll submit a quick ownership verification: your name as owner (or the actual owner's name, if you're not the owner), the property's location, and a deed/volume number. If you're not the owner, you'll also upload a notarized MOU document.

This goes through the same three-stage review as host applications (Product Support → Compliance, which may include a site inspection → Supervisor). You can track its status right on the Properties tab; if corrections are requested, you'll see the reviewer's notes and a button to resubmit. A listing can't be published or booked until verification is approved.

💬 Getting Help

Chat with our assistant

The chat bubble in the bottom corner of every page can answer common questions about bookings, payments, becoming a host, and more, using our current listings and help content. If it can't answer, or you ask to speak to a person, it hands off to our support team and opens a support ticket for you automatically.

Support tickets

For anything the chat can't resolve, open Support: pick a category (Account, Booking, Payment & Refunds, Listing, Safety, Technical, Host Support, Other), describe your issue, and attach files if helpful. You don't need an account to submit one, but logging in lets you track it under My Tickets, where you can see status (Open, In Progress, Awaiting You, Resolved, Closed) and reply to our team.

Reporting a listing, host, or review

Use the Report button on any listing, host profile, or review to flag scams, fake listings, inappropriate content, harassment, or incorrect information. Our Trust & Safety team reviews every report.

💳 Payments & Fees

FeeWhat it's for
Service feeA percentage of your stay's subtotal, shown as its own line at checkout.
Booking feeA small flat fee applied to bookings.
Viewing feeA small flat, non-refundable fee to schedule a viewing on a long-term rental.
MoMo transaction feeA separate ~2% fee charged by MTN on Mobile Money payments (not a HomeKonet fee).

You can save a card for faster checkout from your Account page — we only ever store the card's last 4 digits, expiry, and cardholder name, never the full card number.

Need a refund? Refunds are reviewed and processed by our team based on the listing's cancellation policy — reach out via Support or message your host to start that conversation.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a host have to confirm my request?

Up to 7 days, unless the listing is Instant Book, in which case it confirms immediately.

When am I actually charged?

Never at the moment you request a booking. You pay only after the booking is confirmed, within a 10-day window.

What payment methods are accepted?

Cards via Stripe, and MTN Mobile Money.

How do I become a host?

Apply from "Become a Host" while logged in, and complete the three-stage review.

Can I message a host before booking?

Yes — use the "Message host" button on any listing page.
